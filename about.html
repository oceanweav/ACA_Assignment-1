HISTORY



Founded in 2010, Our Campus began as a small community initiative aimed at empowering local youth through education and practical skills. What started with a handful of passionate educators and a single classroom has grown into a vibrant learning institution recognized for excellence in community engagement and health training.
From the beginning, our mission has been clearto equip students with knowledge that makes a real difference in people’s lives. Over the years, the campus has expanded its facilities, modernized its classrooms, and strengthened partnerships with local organizations, hospitals, and community groups.

In 2015, the campus launched its Community Service Program, designed to encourage students to actively participate in volunteer work and local development projects. This program became a cornerstone of our educational approach, fostering empathy, teamwork, and leadership among our learners.

By 2018, seeing the growing need for safety and emergency preparedness, the campus introduced its First Aid Training Course. The program quickly gained recognition for its hands-on, practical teaching methods that empower students to respond confidently in real-life emergencies.

Today, Our Campus stands as a hub of learning and service — a place where education meets compassion. With dedicated instructors, supportive staff, and a strong community spirit, the campus continues to grow, adapt, and inspire future generations to learn, serve, and make a lasting impact.
